ScoreWatch

Keep track of your score!

ScoreWatch is an app that helps sportsmen and gamers to keep track of their score in a quick way while they are playing.

This app is specifically made for WatchOS, it is meant to be timely and concise, the user can access it quickly and within two touches he will be able to get to the core of the app and will be ready to get into action. ScoreWatch is for amatorial players so they won't lose games anymore because of distraction!
